LC 1581 — Customer Who Visited but Did Not Make Any Transactions
Problem Statement

Two tables:

Visits → Every customer visit.
Transactions → Purchases made during a visit.

Find customers who visited but didn't buy anything. Return:

customer_id
Number of visits where they didn't make a transaction.
Core Idea
LEFT JOIN Visits with Transactions using visit_id.
Find visits where no matching transaction exists (NULL after the join).
GROUP BY customer_id and COUNT() those visits.

Think of it like this:

Visit 1 → Bought something ✅
Visit 2 → No purchase ❌
Visit 3 → No purchase ❌

Answer: Customer has 2 visits without transactions.

This problem mainly tests:

LEFT JOIN
Finding NULL after a JOIN
GROUP BY + COUNT()
